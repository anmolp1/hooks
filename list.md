# Good hooks
* Everyone tells you to [common generic advice] but nobody actually tells you HOW to do it
* Is it possible to [achieve dream result] while [relatable constraint] in [specific timeframe]?
* If you are a [broad identity] and you want to never [painful problem] again, watch this
* If I woke up [specific painful situation] tomorrow and wanted to [dream result] by [timeframe], here's exactly what I would do
* here's exactly how you are going to [specific action] if you want to [desired identity/outcome]
* Over the past [timeframe], I have grown my [thing] from [before] to [after]
* It took me [long timeframe] to learn this, but I will teach it to you in less than [short timeframe]
* If you are in your [20s - age group], here are [number] things you need to make sure you don't end up [painful outcome] by [age group]

# Bad hooks
* How to [do the generic thing]
* How to [achieve result] while [doing other thing]
* [vague topic] for [audience] who are [problem]
* My approach to [topic]
* How to [vague motivation] for [becoming something]
* How I grew my [thing]
* What I learned about [topic] over [time period]
* [number] tips for [age group] [creators/people]
